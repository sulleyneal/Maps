Ruston

A railroad town of 1884 in the piney hills of North Louisiana — live terrain, streets & landmarks.

Founded when the Vicksburg, Shreveport & Pacific line crossed Robert E. Russ’s land, Ruston grew from depot to parish seat within a year. Today it’s a university town of 22,000 — Louisiana Tech on the west side, Grambling State four miles further — known statewide for June peaches and singletrack in the hills.
